Adult Hockey League

TWIN RINKS ADULT HOCKEY

New Players Always Welcome! Join anytime

Email: adulthockey@twinrinks.com

We need Goalies! Email: goalies@twinrinks.com

Our leagues are made up of individuals (not teams) and are formed by a draft to attempt to balance the teams within each league. Most of our players are over 30 and many over 50. It's a little different at this rink in that we do have very competitive hockey but, the focus is on fun, exercise, safe play, good sportsmanship and cold drinks. Not winning or losing. There is lots of subbing (free hockey for rostered players), so sub a lot!

We have 7 levels of play. Starting with Instructional classes designed for individuals with minimal or no previous hockey experience who want to develop their skills and learn to play the game. The goal is for each participant to graduate from instructional classes and join our beginner hockey league. Our highest leagues is our Diamond league with individuals playing at a high C or low B level.

We add new players every day. Full equipment including a helmet with full face covering required.
